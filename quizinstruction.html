Quizzes Instructions

1. The quiz has 10 questions.
2. You have to complete the quiz in 10 minutes.
3. The quiz will show one question at a time.
4. You can't move to the next question before selecting an answer. Only one choice can be selected.
5. When you answer the last question, the Submit Button will appear and pressing it will show your grade.
6. When you finish the quiz, you can see the correct and wrong answers.
